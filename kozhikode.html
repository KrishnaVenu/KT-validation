TOURIST PLACES IN KOZHIKODE

PAYYOLI BEACH

The Payyoli Beach looks extremely beautiful with its groves of swaying coconut palms and long strectches of silver sands, bordered by beach shacks that offer decious snacks and sea food to the tourists. Thikkodi light house is a prime attraction here, built following a shipwreck and if you go about a kilometer and a half north of Payyoli, you will find a beautiful sand bank at the estuary of River Mpprad.

ARIPPARA WATERFALLS

Located near Nellipoyil in Thiruvambadi panchayat of Kozhikode district.Not one of the most popular Waterfalls in Kerala, but absolutely one of the most beautiful river picnic spot. The waterfall is on one of the tributaries of Iruvanji River commonly known as Iruvanjipuzha. A pool is formed at bottom layer of this long cascading falls which is a good place for swimming.

KARIYATHUMPARA

Kariyathumpara is a tourist destination for the viewers who loved the real beauty of the nature, such a lovely tourist spot located at the heart of kozhikode district, kozhikode is commonly known as dolphin point. there are many other tourist attraction lies in the district. such a beautiful destination situated along with the western ghats in wayanad.

SARGAALAYA

Located amid the picturesque village of Iringal, a wonderful world of tradition in the form of finest handicrafts – this exquisite blend of tradition with culture, Sargaalaya has got it all to bestow a unique and joyful experience for every visitor.The village also has recreational boating facility, as it is situated on the banks of the Moorad River. Both rowing and paddle boats are available.
