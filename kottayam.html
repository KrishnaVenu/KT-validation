TOURIST PLACES IN KOTTAYAM

AYODHYA HOUSE BOAT

Overlooking the backwaters , Hotel Ayodhya Cruize offers all meals to its patrons. The property is accessible from the Kodathi Bus Stop, which is 2 km away.The houseboat is inclusive of seven well-maintained and spacious rooms that are equipped with essential requisites. Available amenities include television set, wardrobe, makeup mirror and attached bathroom with hot/cold running water.

CLAY ART CAFE

Clay Art Cafe is a quaint art gallery cum cafetaria situated on the K. K. Road in Kottayam. The building has been made of mud and old style bamboo thatch roofing. On demand you can also witness live clay modelling. Popular for its delicious milkshakes and coffees, the cafe also sells local art works and paintings.Clay Art Cafe is open: Sun - Sun 3:00 PM - 9:00 PM, Mon - Sat 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM

ELAVEEZHAPOONCHIRA

Ilaveezhapoonchira is a tourist destination located in Melukavu village in Kottayam district near Kanjar. Ilaveezha Poonchira is surrounded by three enchanting hillocks - Mankunnu, Kodayathoormala and Thonippara. This makes the place ideal for trekking. Ilavvezha Poonchira is located 55 km from Kottayam, and is 20 km from Thodupuzha. It is one of the places in Kerala where you can watch sunrise and sunset.

ILLIKKAL KALLU

Monolith located on top of the Illickal Malaa in the Kottayam district of Kerala, India.The view from illikal kallu is mesmerizing with numerous hills rising to the clouds and the deep valleys. Illikkal Mala comprises three hills, each rising up to 3,400 ft above sea level. Each of the hills has a peculiar shape. From the hilltops, the Arabian Sea can be seen on the far horizon as a thin blue stroke. The nearest town is Teekoy.
