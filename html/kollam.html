TOURIST PLACES IN KOLLAM

JATAYU'S EARTH CENTRE

Jatayu Earth’s Center, also known as Jatayu Nature Park or Jatayu Rock, is a park and tourism centre at Chadayamangalam in Kollam district of Kerala. It stands at an altitude of 350m above mean sea level.The monumental statue of Jatayu resting high up the hill catches your attention even from afar on your way to Chadayamangalam in Kollam district. Jatayu is a legendary bird from the Indian epic Ramayana.

KUMBHAVURUTHY WATERFALLS

Kumbhavurutty Waterfall is a celebrated waterfalls in South India arranged close Aryankavu panchayath in Kollam region of Kerala close Tamil Nadu outskirt. Situated on the eastern region of Kollam District, one among the few pack pulling waterfalls in Kerala.Kumbhavurutty falls is one among the few crowd pulling waterfalls in Kerala.The travelers can see wild animals also if lucky. Because the waterfall is very close to the thick Konni Forest area.

MUNREO-ISLAND

Munroe Island is a hidden pearl in the backwaters which is composed of a cluster of 8 islands. Each of them is separated by small water channels and lakes. Munroe Island is located about 27 km from Kollam. The place is named in honour of Resident Colonel John Munroe, of the former princely state of Travancore.It offers a unique and tranquil setting, far away from the constant chaos of modern life.

THANGASSERY LIGHT HOUSE

Also known as the Tangasseri Light House, boasts of being the second tallest on the entire coast of Kerala and is thus even one of the most visited lighthouses in the state on this account. Located at Tangasseri in the city of Kollam, it is one of the two lighthouses in the Kollam Metropolitan Area and is presently under the surveillance of the Chennai Directorate General of Lighthouses and Lightships.
